Moscow Mule

Summer Fun Refreshing Moscow Mule

Light and refreshing for those hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • Unflavored Vodka
  • Lime
  • Ice
  • Trader Joe's Ginger Ale
  • Mint leaves
  • Copper Cups

Steps

  1. Chill your copper cups in the refrigerator until nice and cold.
  2. Get your copper cups and fill with favorite ice.
  3. Add lime juice to cups.
  4. Add vodka to cups.
  5. Pour in ginger ale.
  6. Garnish with mint leaves and lime wedge.
